Descrição

The House On Holland Hill is a 15-20 minute first-person narrative game in which you play a food delivery guy. Through a series of vignettes, you cross paths with the residents of a beautiful house on Holland Hill road, where you make frequent deliveries. In the wake of tragedy, something ominous looms behind their front door.

Featuring:

  • 3D environment to wander around in
  • Hand-painted 2D characters to meet
  • A game length that won't waste your time
  • 13 deliveries to make
  • Four possible endings to arrive at
  • Peep on the lives of the inhabitants of the house
  • Get involved or remain professional
  • Find out what's going on behind closed doors
  • Navigate interactive dialogues
  • Keyboard and controller support

Originally made during the Idle Thumbs Wizard Jam 4.
