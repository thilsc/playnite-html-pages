Descrição

Two of the most beloved Disney games of all time make their long-awaited return to modern consoles in Disney Classic Games: Aladdin and The Lion King!
The bundle contains different versions of these two classics:
Aladdin is playable in the Mega Drive / Genesis Version (US & Japan) and Game Boy (including a colorised variant).
Lion King is playable as Super Nintendo, Mega Drive / Genesis Version (US & Japan) and Game Boy (including a colorised variant).
