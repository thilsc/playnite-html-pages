Descrição

The detective prodigy Jam already knows who did the crime, but what the crime actually was remains to be determined.

It's up to you to guide Jam through eight rooms of point-and-click puzzles, gather evidence, and deduce what really happened in the Mystery of the Mysteriously Spooky Mansion.

Features

  • 30+ minutes of point-and-click mystery gameplay
  • 15 different endings (plus an extra secret one)
  • At least two or three jokes
  • Includes download of original soundtrack (2 songs)

Content warning: PG-13 swearing; mild body horror; some unpleasant images. No jump scares.
Accessibility: Font select; text speed slider.

Jam and the Mystery of the Mysteriously Spooky Mansion was made by Res, an indie game developer working from British Columbia, Canada.
