Descrição

Ordell Harrison, a guy in his twenties, is just trying to get through the day, but it's not enough anymore. After his mother dies, he moves to a different town to start over, but things don’t seem to be getting any better for him.

Ordell needs friends, and maybe someone he can confide in. Guide him through a rough patch in his life and help him make connections again. There's 5 people he can choose from.

He just needs to make the first step.

~~~

Talk to Me is a Visual Novel about grief, mental health and friendship intended for audiences over the age of 18. Please note there are no explicit images in the game. This can be a hard game to play if you’re depressed or have just lost someone close to you, so please be aware of that.

Content Warnings:

  • Alcohol & Drug Use
  • Mental Health themes
  • Mentions of Abusive Relationships
  • Mentions of Suicide Attempts
  • Parental Death
  • Verbal Descriptions of Sexual Acts
  • Domestic/Relationship Violence

For more detailed content warnings, please view the following. Please note that this list contains spoilers:

Detailed Content Warnings
  • Descriptions of sudden parental death
  • Clinically depressed main character
  • Descriptions of homophobia and homophobic language
  • Description of absent parents
  • Mentions of abortion
  • Depictions of abusive partner, with violence both physical and emotional
  • Depictions and Descriptions of drug use/addiction


Features:

  • There are no 100% good or bad endings in this game. You won’t get a game over. There’s no true ending either
  • Over 75k words of story, with difficult choices and consequences
  • A vibrant cast of characters
  • Explore up to 20 different outcomes of the game. See how your choices have affected Ordell’s life
  • 25+BGs and 10+ CGs
  • 5 Love Interests (4F, 1M)
