Descrição

"WOWOWOW!"


WOWOWOW KORONE BOX is a fanmade hololive top-down shooter featuring Inugami Korone, member of hololive GAMERS! Collect Korone Boxes to increase your score and equip random weapons, while fending off ever-increasing numbers of enemies and various bosses. Take one hit and it's game over, so exercise your dodging skills to the limit! More weapons can be unlocked by collecting more boxes in total or reaching higher scores! All the while, jam out to arrangements of various official and fanmade tunes.

WARNING: This game contains some flashing lights and pixelated depictions of insects. Player discretion is advised.
